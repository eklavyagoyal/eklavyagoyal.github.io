About Me

I am a Bachelor’s student at the Technical University of Munich (TUM), currently pursuing my degree in Physics. In parallel with my studies, I work as a Machine Learning Assistant at Tools for Humanity, where I contribute to the development of machine learning models aimed at addressing global challenges.

Current Role Link to heading

At Tools for Humanity, my work focuses on data preprocessing, model development, and optimization for machine learning applications. I collaborate with researchers and developers to build systems that can efficiently process and analyze large datasets, enabling impactful solutions.
